FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started. Can't find what you're looking for? Talk to our team.

What is a Merchant of Record? +
A Merchant of Record is the entity legally responsible for selling your products to end customers. Rayl Pay takes on the financial, legal, and compliance responsibilities — meaning we handle tax collection, chargebacks, fraud liability, and regulatory requirements on your behalf.
How long does onboarding take? +
Most businesses are live in under 48 hours. Our no-code overlay checkout can go live in minutes. Full API integrations typically take 1–3 days depending on your stack.
Do you handle VAT and sales tax automatically? +
Yes, completely. We calculate, collect, and remit VAT, GST, and sales tax in 200+ jurisdictions automatically. You never have to think about tax compliance again.
What payment methods are supported? +
Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, JCB, Diners Club, Maestro, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, bank transfers, and local payment methods in 45+ currencies. We add new methods regularly.
How are payouts scheduled? +
You choose your payout schedule — daily, weekly, or monthly. Funds arrive via bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA/ACH) with full transaction transparency and reporting in your dashboard.
