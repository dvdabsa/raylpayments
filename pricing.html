A transaction is any successful charge — where a customer's payment is authorised, captured, and settled. Failed payments, refunds, and disputes are not counted as transactions. This means you only pay when you actually get paid, which is exactly how it should be.

As your Merchant of Record, Rayl Pay is legally responsible for collecting and remitting all applicable taxes — VAT, GST, sales tax — across 200+ jurisdictions worldwide. We calculate the correct tax rate at checkout, collect it from the customer, and file and remit it to the relevant authorities automatically. You never touch it. Your dashboard always shows a clean breakdown of tax collected vs. revenue retained.
RaylPay supports 45+ currencies for customer-facing checkout, including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, SGD, INR, BRL, and many more. Customers pay in their local currency; you receive payouts in your preferred currency. FX conversion is handled at competitive rates and is included in your 5% + 50¢ — no hidden FX markup.
You are never charged for failed transactions. For subscription products, our dunning system handles automated recovery emails and retry schedules, all configurable from your dashboard.
